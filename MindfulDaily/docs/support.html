Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track an activity?

Simply tap on any activity card on the home screen. Each activity has its own tracking method:

  • Exercise: Enter duration and type of exercise
  • Meditation: Use the built-in timer or skip to mark complete
  • Gratitude: Add items you're grateful for
  • Outdoor Time: Select activity and duration
  • Sleep: Enter your sleep and wake times

What are streaks?

Streaks track how many consecutive days you've completed all five activities. Your current streak and best streak are displayed on the home screen to keep you motivated!

How do I view my progress?

Tap the chart icon in the top-right corner of the home screen to see your weekly summary. This shows your completion rate for each day of the week.

Is my data private?

Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Your wellness journey is completely private.

Can I use the app offline?

Absolutely! MindfulDaily works completely offline. No internet connection is required to track your activities or view your progress.

How do I reset my data?

You can clear all app data by uninstalling and reinstalling the app. Note that this action cannot be undone.

Tips for Success

  • Set a daily reminder to check in with the app
  • Start small - even 5 minutes of meditation counts!
  • Use the gratitude journal to reflect on positive moments
  • Track your sleep consistently for better insights
  • Celebrate your streaks - every day counts!

Troubleshooting

The app isn't saving my activities

Make sure to tap the "Save" or "Complete" button after entering your activity details. The activity card should show a green checkmark when completed.

My streak reset unexpectedly

Streaks reset if you miss completing all five activities in a day. Make sure to complete all activities before midnight to maintain your streak.
